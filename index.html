About Me

关于我

My research focuses on urban-rural development, land use patterns, and spatial planning theory and practice.

我的研究聚焦于城乡发展与土地利用、国土空间规划理论与实践。

In the field of urban-rural development and land-use studies, my research primarily focuses on the differentiated patterns of land use among counties under rapid urbanization. By adopting a comparative research approach, I emphasize the “actions” of diverse stakeholders as a key analytical dimension. This perspective aims to extend existing theoretical frameworks on urban and rural land use, and to deepen the understanding of localized land institutions and spatial governance mechanisms.

在城乡发展和土地利用研究领域，我重点关注快速城市化进程中县域之间土地利用模式的差异及其成因。通过比较研究的方法，特别强调多元行为主体的“行动”维度，旨在拓展现有城乡土地利用理论框架，并深化对地方土地制度与空间治理机制的理解。

In terms of territorial spatial planning, my work explores how interdisciplinary theories can support the construction of territorial spatial planning systems. Recently, I have been particularly engaged in the monitoring and evaluation of plan implementation. By integrating spatial analysis with causal inference methods, I seek to advance the methodological foundations for evaluating territorial spatial planning practices.

在国土空间规划研究方面，我致力于探索跨学科理论在国土空间规划体系构建中的支撑作用。近期研究主要聚焦于国土空间规划实施监测与评估，尝试将空间分析方法与因果推断技术相结合，以推动国土空间规划评估方法论的创新与发展。
