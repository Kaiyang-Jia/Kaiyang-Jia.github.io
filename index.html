About Me

关于我

My research focuses on urban-rural development, land use patterns, and spatial planning theory and practice.

我的研究聚焦于城乡发展与土地利用、国土空间规划理论与实践。

In urban-rural development research, I specifically examine the causes of differentiated land use patterns among counties during rapid urbanization. Through comparative research methods and stakeholder interactions, I aim to expand existing theoretical frameworks of urban-rural land use.

在城乡发展研究中，我特别关注快速城市化进程中县域间差异化土地利用模式的成因。通过比较研究方法，行为主体互动，对现有城乡土地利用理论框架进行拓展。

In spatial planning, I focus on how interdisciplinary theories support the construction of spatial planning systems. My recent work centers on implementation monitoring and evaluation of spatial plans, attempting to combine spatial analysis with causal inference to introduce new evaluation methodologies for spatial planning.

国土空间规划方面，我聚焦于跨学科理论对国土空间规划体系构建的支撑。近期工作主要围绕国土空间规划的实施监测与评估，尝试将空间分析与因果推断相结合，引入国土空间规划评估方法论。
